Kevin Walters

Caribbean Soul & Sound

The Artist

Drawing deeply from his Caribbean roots, Kevin Walters weaves intricate narratives of life, love, and rhythm. His sound is a magical blend of traditional acoustics and modern, atmospheric production.

Whether he's performing an intimate acoustic set or commanding a festival stage, his music invites listeners into a warm, captivating world of emotion and authenticity.
